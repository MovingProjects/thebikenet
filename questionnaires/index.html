theBIKEnet questionnaires

This page lists all official questionnaires used in the theBIKEnet project. The instruments cover profile, travel habits, safety perception, safety behaviour, urban context, infrastructure conditions, mobility services, attitudes, accidents and risk factors, and trip diaries.

Each card shows: bilingual title, description, a link to complete the survey (when an active campaign is available) and direct access to the JSON definition for transparency and research reuse.
