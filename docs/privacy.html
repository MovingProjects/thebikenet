Data Controller

The data controller is Moving Projects Srl, Via Labicana 50, 00184, Rome, Italy – info@movprojects.com.

Categories of Data Collected

  • Access data: email and password (authentication only)
  • Mobility data: GPS traces
  • Survey data: profile, habits, perceptions
  • Reports: geolocated safety notes
  • Technical metadata: timestamp, device type

Purpose of Processing

Data are collected for scientific research on cycling and micromobility.

  • Analyse cycling behaviour
  • Evaluate infrastructure
  • Create safety indicators
  • Publish anonymized datasets

Legal Basis and Research Framework

The legal basis is GDPR Article 89 (processing for scientific research).

Data Storage and Access Levels

  • Access data: encrypted and separated
  • Mobility/survey data: pseudonymized
  • Aggregated data: anonymized
  • Scientific datasets: shared under license

Retention Period

Data are kept for up to 5 years after project closure.

Rights of Participants

  • Access, correction, deletion
  • Information on processing

Contact: info@movprojects.com
