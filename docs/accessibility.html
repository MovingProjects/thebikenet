Accessibility Statement

This statement applies to the website of theBIKEnet (https://movingprojects.github.io/thebikenet/), developed by Moving Projects Srl within the PlanEasy Crowdsourcing Framework.

1. Commitment to Accessibility

Moving Projects Srl is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience and applying the relevant accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 and 2.2 Level AA, EN 301 549).

2. Compliance Status

The website partially conforms to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA. Non-conformities and exemptions are described below.

3. Non-Accessible Content

The following areas may still present accessibility issues:

  • Some logos and decorative images may lack empty alt attributes.
  • Minor contrast variations on certain text–background combinations.
  • PDF or external linked documents may not yet be fully tagged for screen readers.

These limitations will be addressed in upcoming revisions of theBIKEnet website and its associated documentation.

4. Preparation of This Statement

This statement was prepared on 6 November 2025. The accessibility of this website was assessed through self-evaluation and automated tools (WAVE, Axe, Lighthouse).

The statement was last reviewed on 6 November 2025.

5. Feedback and Contact Information

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact:

  • Email: info@movprojects.com
  • Organisation: Moving Projects Srl, Via Labicana 50, 00184 Rome, Italy

6. Enforcement Procedure (EU Directive 2016/2102)

In accordance with Article 9 of the EU Directive 2016/2102, if you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility feedback, you may submit a complaint to the national enforcement body:

Agenzia per l’Italia Digitale (AgID)
https://www.agid.gov.it/
Email: ufficio.accessibilita@agid.gov.it
