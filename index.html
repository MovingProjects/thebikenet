Our vision

theBIKEnet is a research initiative designed to connect everyday cycling experience with urban data. We study how people move, how they perceive safety and comfort, and what influences their choices when travelling by bike or micromobility. Our goal is to support cities in becoming safer, more accessible, and more cycling-friendly.

What we do

We collect stories and real-world data from everyday riders: GPS traces, trip contexts, perceptions, habits, and attitudes. By combining these with spatial and infrastructural indicators, we build a detailed picture of how cycling is experienced across the city.
